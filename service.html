Key Focus Areas of Training

Judicial Processes and Legal Systems

With over two decades of experience as a judge and legal consultant, our team can provide in-depth training on judicial processes, administrative law, and conflict resolution. Participants gain insights into effective dispute resolution strategies and understanding legal frameworks to navigate Ethiopia’s judicial system.

Trade, Investment, and ADR Training

Our team can bring expertise in trade, investment, and engineering contracts to our training sessions. Key topics include:

Drafting and negotiating infrastructure agreements.

Understanding arbitration and alternative dispute resolution (ADR) mechanisms.

Compliance with regulatory frameworks for foreign direct investment (FDI).

Empowering NGOs for Social Justice: Training and Consultancy Program

This program, led by our experienced legal and social justice expert, the program aims to strengthen the capacity of NGOs to address gender-based violence (GBV), child protection, and refugee rights. It offers targeted training modules on GBV awareness, child advocacy, refugee protection, and policy influence, alongside consultancy services for program development, stakeholder capacity building, and research-driven advocacy.

NGOs will gain tools and strategies to design impactful programs, enhance collaboration, and influence policy for sustainable change. Delivered through in-person workshops, online training, or tailored consultancy, the program is designed to empower NGOs with the expertise needed to safeguard vulnerable populations and drive meaningful social justice initiatives.

Tax Law and Corporate Compliance

Our team’s academic and practical background provides specialized training on tax laws and corporate compliance, including:

Legal representation in disputes involving taxation and investment.

Policies to promote fair business practices and safeguard vulnerable populations.

Tax planning for businesses.

Environmental Law and Governance

Leveraging our partner’s extensive experience, they will lead sessions on:

Environmental impact assessments and audits.

Natural resource governance and sustainable practices.

Leveraging legal frameworks for ecological protection and climate action advocacy.

Customizable Training Modules Legal Literacy Workshops: Simplifying complex legal concepts for community groups, businesses, and NGOs.

Corporate Legal Compliance Programs: Helping organizations adhere to regulatory requirements in areas like tax, investment, and labor laws.

Policy and Advocacy Training: Equipping policymakers and advocates with skills to influence legal reforms and environmental governance.

Professional Development for Legal Practitioners: Advanced training on arbitration, legislative drafting, and public interest litigation.
